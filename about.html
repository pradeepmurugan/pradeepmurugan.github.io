My Approach

I follow a human-centered design approach, starting with understanding the users' needs and pain points. Every project begins with research and discovery, followed by ideation, prototyping, and iterative testing. I believe in creating designs that are not only visually appealing but also functional, accessible, and delightful to use.

Collaboration is key to my process. I work closely with stakeholders, developers, and other team members to ensure that the final product meets both business goals and user needs. I'm always open to feedback and believe that the best solutions come from diverse perspectives and continuous iteration.
